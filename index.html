Why Bosch Industry 4.0

The power of Industry 4.0 is becoming real – and Bosch Rexroth is uniquely prepared to help machine builders and manufacturers make the vision of the Smart Factory into reality today.

Rexroth is driving the next industrial revolution as both a lead operator and lead supplier of Industry 4.0 solutions.
